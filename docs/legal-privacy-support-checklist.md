# Legal, Privacy, And Support Checklist

This is a technical source-material checklist. It is not legal advice and is not legally sufficient without human/legal review.

## Existing Technical Privacy Surfaces

- Accounts/authentication and sessions.
- Profiles, posts, comments, and follows.
- Messages, media attachments, pins, saves, forwarding, search, polls, events, and actions.
- Moments and viewer/audience behavior.
- Communities and community posts.
- Reels upload, playback, comments, likes, and recommendations.
- Discovery and For You recommendations.
- Media upload, scanning, approval, rejection, quarantine, and deletion states.
- Moderation, reports, blocks, and safety controls.
- Account export and deletion behavior.
- Push device registration and token handling.
- AI isolation and mock/fallback boundaries.
- Cookies, local storage, and SecureStore usage.

## Missing User-Facing Policies

Each item requires human/legal review:

- Privacy Policy.
- Terms of Service.
- Community Guidelines.
- Acceptable Use Policy.
- IP/DMCA process where relevant.
- Minimum-age policy.
- Support contact and process.
- Security contact.
- Data retention explanation.
- Push notification explanation.
- Account deletion instructions.

## Store Privacy Source Material Needed

- Data categories collected.
- Data linked to user identity.
- Data used for recommendations.
- Data shared with processors/providers.
- Retention and deletion behavior.
- Push notification purpose.
- Media processing/scanning behavior.
- AI provider behavior if enabled.

## Support Readiness

- Do not use placeholder support addresses as live support.
- A real support contact, escalation path, and incident communication owner must be chosen before launch.
