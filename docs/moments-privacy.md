# Moments Privacy

Moments are short-lived text or photo updates shared with the audience selected at creation time. Moments expire after 24 hours.

Audience choices:

- Contacts
- Contacts except...
- Only share with...
- Close Friends Moments

Contacts are derived from active direct chats. Blocked users, deleted accounts, deactivated accounts, group-only participants, and inaccessible chats are excluded. Block changes are also checked when a Moment is viewed.

Photo Moments use the approved media upload pipeline. Viewers receive photo bytes only through the authorized Moment media endpoint, not through raw media IDs.

Moment viewers are visible only to the author. Moment archive is private to the author and can be disabled in Privacy settings.

Moments are not chat messages. They are excluded from chat search, global message search, forwarding, Shared Media, Shared Documents, Shared Links, Action extraction, and AI/Group Brain context.
